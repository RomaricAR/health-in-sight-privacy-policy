Health In-Sight Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 09, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Health In-Sight. This application is designed to help you track your health metrics including sleep, steps, calories, distance, resting heart rate, and heart rate variability. By using this app, you agree to comply with and be bound by the following privacy policy. Please review it carefully.

2. Data Usage and Privacy

Health In-Sight does not collect, store, or share any of your personal health data. All data tracked by the app remains on your device and is used solely for display purposes to provide you with comprehensive health insights. We do not have access to your data and it is not transmitted to any servers or third parties.

HealthKit Data Usage:

  • We use Apple's HealthKit framework to access and display your health data.
  • The app only reads data types that you have explicitly granted permission for in the app settings.
  • We do not modify or write any data to HealthKit.
  • Your HealthKit data is not shared with any third parties or used for advertising purposes.
  • You can revoke the app's access to your HealthKit data at any time through your device's Settings app.

3. User Responsibilities

As a user of Health In-Sight, you agree to:

  • Provide accurate and complete information for accurate tracking.
  • Use the app for personal health tracking purposes only.
  • Not misuse the app in any way that could harm its functionality or other users.

4. Limitation of Liability

Health In-Sight is provided as a tool for health tracking and should not be considered a substitute for professional medical advice. We are not liable for any health issues that may arise from the use of this app. Always consult with a healthcare provider for medical advice and concerns.

The app does not provide any health recommendations or diagnoses. It only displays data retrieved from HealthKit and allows you to set personal goals.

5. Changes to Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or change our privacy policy at any time. We will notify users of any significant changes through the app or via email. Continued use of the app following the posting of changes to this policy will mean you accept those changes.
